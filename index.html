Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about WorkInSite

How do I create my first site in WorkInSite?

^

Creating your first site is simple:
1) Open the WorkInSite app.
2) Tap the yellow "New Site" button on your dashboard.
3) Fill in the site details including name, location, and description
4) Set up your team members and their roles,
5) Configure attendance settings.
6) Save your site. Your new site will appear on your dashboard immediately.

Can employees check in from their personal phones?

^

Yes! Employees can download the WorkInSite mobile app on their personal iOS or Android devices. Once logged in with their credentials, they can check in and out from job sites using GPS verification. The app also supports facial recognition for secure attendance tracking.

How do I generate attendance reports?

^

Navigate to the Reports section in your dashboard, select the date range you want to review, choose specific sites or employees if needed, and click "Generate Report." You can export reports in PDF, Excel, or CSV formats. Reports include check-in/out times, total hours worked, and any exceptions or late arrivals.

Is WorkInSite available offline?

^

WorkInSite offers limited offline functionality. Employees can check in and out even without internet connection, and the data will sync automatically once connectivity is restored. However, real-time reporting and some administrative features require an active internet connection.

What support options are available?

^

We offer multiple support channels: 24/7 live chat support through the app, email support with response within 24 hours, comprehensive documentation and video tutorials in our Help Center, and phone support during business hours for premium plan subscribers. We also provide onboarding assistance for new customers.
